# ABS REITs Evidence

## Use When

Use for asset-backed securities, public REITs, asset-pool cash flow, tranche design, waterfall, credit enhancement, project NOI, valuation, and secondary liquidity.

## Source Reports

- `cicc-abs-framework-2025`
- `cicc-public-reits-strategy-2025`
- `huatai-credit-bond-framework-2025`

## Core Framework Claims

- ABS analysis requires bottom-up asset-pool performance, cash-flow waterfall, tranche priority, credit enhancement, servicer quality, legal isolation, valuation, and liquidity.
- Public REITs analysis requires project NOI, occupancy, rent, capex, distribution, leverage, sponsor, asset valuation, and secondary-market liquidity.
- Seniority or distribution yield does not eliminate asset, operation, prepayment, legal, valuation, or liquidity risk.

## Data Fields Mentioned

- Asset pool balance, default/prepayment, cash-flow date, waterfall, tranche, subordination, trigger, enhancement, servicer.
- Project NOI, occupancy, rent, capex, distribution yield, leverage, sponsor, asset valuation, secondary turnover.
- Rates curve, credit spread, comparable yield, liquidity premium, discount/premium, disclosure frequency.

## Common Misreadings

- Treating senior ABS tranches as risk-free.
- Comparing REITs distribution yield with bond yield without growth, capex, and asset-value assumptions.
- Ignoring servicer, concentration, prepayment, and disclosure risk.
- Treating structured priority as a substitute for asset-pool due diligence.
