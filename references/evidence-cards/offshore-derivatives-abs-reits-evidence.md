## Core Framework Claims

- Offshore debt analysis separates global risk-free rates, FX/hedge cost, issuer credit, legal terms, and liquidity.
- Interest-rate derivatives connect cash-bond curves, funding cost, futures/swap pricing, basis, IRR, and hedge objectives.
- ABS and REITs require bottom-up cash-flow, structure, legal, and liquidity analysis.

## Data Fields Mentioned

- UST curve, Fed path, DXY, USD/CNH, hedge cost, issuer spread, rating, offshore liquidity.
- CTD, conversion factor, basis, IRR, repo, futures price, swap curve, DV01.
- Asset pool, cash-flow waterfall, tranche priority, credit enhancement, NOI, occupancy, distribution yield.

## Common Misreadings

- Applying onshore implicit-support assumptions directly to offshore debt.
- Mixing directional derivatives trades with hedging.
- Treating senior ABS tranches as risk-free.
- Comparing REITs distribution yield with bond yield without asset, capex, and valuation assumptions.
