# Convertible Hybrid Evidence

## Use When

Use for convertible bonds, exchangeable bonds, parity, conversion premium, bond floor, clauses, hybrid capital instruments, and structured hybrids.

## Source Reports

- `cicc-convertible-bonds-framework-2025`
- `huatai-convertible-framework-2025`
- `cicc-hybrid-assets-framework-2025`

## Core Framework Claims

- Convertible value balances equity upside, bond-floor protection, option value, liquidity, and path-dependent clauses.
- Parity zone determines whether equity beta, premium valuation, bond floor, or clause events dominate.
- Hybrid assets need term-specific analysis; convertible shortcuts do not automatically apply to exchangeables, preferred shares, mixed notes, or structured notes.

## Data Fields Mentioned

- Stock price, conversion price, parity, conversion premium, bond floor, YTM, implied volatility.
- Redemption trigger, put trigger, downward revision status, maturity, coupon, rating, guarantee.
- Underlying equity volatility, liquidity, sector beta, financial quality, and market style.

## Common Misreadings

- Comparing conversion premium without controlling for parity.
- Treating bond floor as protection when issuer credit or liquidity is impaired.
- Ignoring forced redemption risk in high-parity convertibles.
- Applying convertible logic to non-convertible hybrid assets without checking terms.
